TV mounting in San Jose

Before getting started, gather the following tools and materials:
  • Power drill with a masonry bit
  • Concrete wall anchors (expansion anchors or sleeve anchors)
  • Lag bolts
  • Stud finder (to check for hidden obstructions)
  • Level
  • Measuring tape
  • Socket wrench
  • Pencil or marker
  • TV mounting bracket
  • Safety goggles and gloves
Step 1: Choose the Right Location

Decide on the best spot for your TV, considering viewing angles and proximity to power outlets. Use a level and measuring tape to mark the exact position where the mounting bracket will go. Ensure it is centered at a comfortable height for viewing.

Step 2: Mark the Drill Points

Hold the mounting bracket against the wall and mark the drill points using a pencil or marker. Make sure the marks are level to prevent a crooked installation.
